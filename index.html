MINNESOTA


This is an image of Minnesota

Minnesota is a beautiful state located in the Midwest region of the United States. It became a state in 1858 making it the 32nd state of the United States of America. Minnesota is the 12th largest state in size and home to over 5.5 million people.

The state is well known by its metropolitan area nicknamed the Twin Cities. The Twin Cities is composed of Minneapolis and the states capital, St. Paul. Click here for a video showing the beautiful Minnesota city, Minneapolis. Most of the population resides in the cities making the remaining portion of the state mostly vast farmland, prairies, and deciduous forests.


Minneapolis skyline rising above famous urban lake, Lake Calhoun.

Overall the states geography is very flat however in the northeastern portion of Minnesota near Lake Superior, Minnesota becomes filled with contrasting hills. One of the best characteristics of Minnesota is its abundance of lake, the state alone has over 10,000; hence its slogan Land of 10,000 Lakes. Minnesota has both warm, sunny summers as well as snowy, blustery winters. The state experiences beautiful fall and spring seasons as well.
